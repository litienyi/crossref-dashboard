Made by Tien Yi Li

This project was conceptualized, coded, and (largely) designed by Tien Yi Li.

Tien Yi is a Candidate Master of Science in Architectural Studies (SMArchS) in the History, Theory, Criticism of Architecture and Art (HTC) program at MIT. His work is broadly in dialogue with literature in Architectural History, China Studies, and Digital Humanities.

You can find out more about Tien Yi through his GitHub, MIT Profile, LinkedIn, and Personal Website.
